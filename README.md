# Eudoria Regional Reference

An interactive reference application for the Eudoria universe - a world of balance, harmony, and divine unity.

## About

Eudoria Regional Reference is a comprehensive worldbuilding guide featuring:

### Eudoria Tab
- **Eudoria Overview** - Introduction to the world
- **Eudora, Mother Nature** - Profile of the Queen of Life
- **Noga Calendar** - The sacred 13-moon lunar system
- **Four Unions** - Wesari, Estara, Sundra, and Norvayn
- **Four Pillars of Wakan** - WakanTech, Star Sanctum, Goldenvault, and Lion's Court
- **All Regions** - Detailed profiles of all 17 regions
- **Timeline** - Complete historical timeline
- **Interactive Quiz** - "Which Region Are You?"

### Eudoric Tab (Divine Realm)
- **Eudraneth Overview** - The ethereal seat of divine law
- **Eudoric Pantheon** - Complete profiles of the gods
- **Eudoric Numerals** - The first written system of the gods
- **Eudoric Letters** - The sacred 26-letter alphabet
- **Eudoric Zodiac** - The 13 zodiac signs created by Vivatasia
- **Laws of Eimes** - Sacred covenant between gods and mortals *(Work in Progress)*
- **War of Gods** - Complete chronicle of the divine conflict
- **Interactive Quiz** - "Which God Are You?"
